Module temperature modelling

\[ T_\mathrm{m} = E \cdot e^{a + b \cdot v} + T_\mathrm{amb} \]
$E$ - Sun irradiance
$v$ - wind speed
$a$ & $b$ - empirically determined coefficients

And some formulae

\[ \begin{aligned} E & = \frac{h c}{\lambda} \\ B (\lambda , T) & = \frac{2 h c^2}{\lambda^5} \cdot \frac{1}{\exp{\frac{h c}{\lambda k_B T}} - 1}\\ \theta (\lambda) & = \frac{\frac{h c}{\lambda_t}}{\frac{h c}{\lambda}} = \frac{\lambda}{\lambda_t}\\ \Theta & = \frac{\int_{0}^{\lambda_t} B (\lambda) \frac{\lambda}{\lambda_t} \,d\lambda}{\int_{0}^{\infty} B (\lambda) \,d\lambda} \end{aligned} \]
